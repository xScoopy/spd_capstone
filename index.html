Why I'm Here

Gaming has been a hobby I tend to invest a ton of time in. Since I was spending so much time gaming, I figured I'd share my experience with others. Throughout my gaming ventures, I've found myself and those I play with experience a wide range of emotions and attitudes, specifically around the multiplayer space. In an attempt to better myself, I took a break from competitive MOBAs to try to figure out WHY these games create such a disparity among players, and what we can do as a community to find a remedy.
